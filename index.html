Miku's Journey

2007

The Birth of Miku

Hatsune Miku was released by Crypton Future Media as a Vocaloid2 voice synthesizer.

2009

First Live Performance

Miku made her first live appearance as a hologram at "Animelo Summer Live".

2014

World Tour

Miku Expo began, bringing hologram concerts to fans around the globe.

2020

Virtual Revolution

During global lockdowns, Miku's virtual concerts brought music and joy to millions.
